About Us

Previously Polygon SAL, Castor Engineers was founded by Fady Warde in Beirut, 1997. The company’s sole objective was and always will be to be a part of the country’s development by delivering the best construction and building services, while respecting its beauty and its values. It has since evolved in many ways, giving life to all sorts of beautiful projects in various areas of the country.

Castor Engineers has accumulated its reputation as one of the most trustworthy Lebanese companies, with a diverse portfolio which spans across many different market sectors.
